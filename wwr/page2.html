SUAREZ RIVER RAFTING

Rafting in Suarez River in San Gil is ideal for lovers of adrenaline, nature and extreme experiences. Departure time for rafting on the Suárez River: Daily at 11 a.m. Level of the rapids of the Suaréz River: III, IV and V. Includes: Round trip transportation from the Hotel/Hostel in San Gil to the rafting starting point, accident insurance, exclusive rafting equipment, professional rafting guides, kayak lifeguards, photos, snack, fried chicken and beer to celebrate the adventure. What clothes should you wear for rafting on the Suárez River: Bathing suit, shirt, closed shoes (do not wear sandals), extra clothes to change into (after the activity), sunscreen and repellent.

FONCE RIVER RAFTING

Rafting on the Fonce River is perfect to spend a fun time with family and friends. The levels of the rapids of the Fonce River are moderate reaching classes II and III. Departure time for rafting on the Fonce River: Flexible every hour in the morning and in the afternoon. Level of the rapids of the Fonce River: II and III. Ages: From 5 years and up. Includes: Round trip transportation from the Hotel / Hostel in San Gil to the starting place of the rafting, insurance against accidents, exclusive equipment for rafting, professional rafting guides and kayak lifeguards. Note: Rafting on the Fonce River does not require experience and is allowed for children and adults. This extreme sport takes place only if the weather conditions allow it.
